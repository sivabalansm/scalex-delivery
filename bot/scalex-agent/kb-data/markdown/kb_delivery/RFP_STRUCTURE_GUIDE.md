## Document Hierarchy Overview

A complete enterprise outsourcing package typically includes:

### 1. Core Framework Documents
- **RFP Instructions**: Rules for vendor responses, evaluation criteria, timeline
- **Term Sheet**: Key commercial terms (used when full MSA not yet drafted)
- **Master Services Agreement (MSA)**: Legal framework governing relationship

### 2. Exhibits (Detailed Terms)

| Topic | Common Exhibit Label | Purpose |
|------|-----------------------|---------|
| Definitions & Acronyms | Exhibit 1 | Standard terminology for contract |
| Statements of Work | Exhibit 2.x | Service-specific scope and requirements |
| Pricing / Financial Provisions | Often Exhibit 3 (varies) | Rate cards, pricing models, invoicing structure |
| Service Levels / Performance Management | Often Exhibit 4 (varies) | SLAs, metrics, service credits, chronic failure |
| Governance | Often Exhibit 5 (varies) | Meeting cadence, escalation, reporting |
| Human Resources | Often Exhibit 6 (varies) | Staff transfer, rebadging terms |
| Third-Party Contracts | Often Exhibit 7 (varies) | Existing vendor agreements to transition |
| Key Personnel | Often Exhibit 8 (varies) | Named roles/individuals, replacement procedures |
| Locations/Sites | Often Exhibit 9 (varies) | Service delivery locations |
| Disaster Recovery | Often Exhibit 10 (varies) | RTO/RPO, testing, DR responsibilities |
| Invoicing & Payment | Often Exhibit 11 (varies) | Billing procedures, timing, disputes |
| Benchmarking | Often Exhibit 12 (varies) | Market comparison rights and adjustments |
| Reports | Often Exhibit 13 (varies) | Reporting deliverables |
| Customer Satisfaction | Often Exhibit 14 (varies) | Survey and feedback requirements |
| Service Tower Demarcation | Often Exhibit 15 (varies) | Scope boundaries between towers |
| Technical Architecture | Often Exhibit 16 (varies) | Technology standards and requirements |
| Termination Assistance | Often Exhibit 17 (varies) | Exit/transition-out procedures |
| Transformation | Often Exhibit 18 (varies) | Change program requirements |

**Important**: Exhibit numbering is not universal across template packages. Always map by **topic and title**, not just the exhibit number. In this specific package, the files labeled “Exhibit 3” and “Exhibit 4” are titled **Performance Management** and **Pricing and Financial Provisions** respectively.

### 3. Appendices (Current State Documentation)

| Appendix | Content |
|----------|---------|
| A | Data Center Overview |

| C | Server Environment |
| D | Network Environment |
| E | End User Computing |
| F | ADM Environment |
| G | Cross-Functional Services |
| H | Human Resources |
| I | Cloud Environment |
