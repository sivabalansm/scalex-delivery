## Part 4: Maxx Roadmap — RFP-Centric Implementation

### 4.1 Savings Framework Overview

Nielsen's HR procurement transformation achieves **$46.1M in annual savings** (36.1% of 2025 baseline) through two complementary approaches:

#### Category 1: Direct RFP Savings ($26.0M / 20.4%)

Savings achieved through competitive RFPs across 10 major spend categories. Each RFP applies three savings pillars:

| Pillar | Description | Mechanism | Total Savings |
|--------|-------------|-----------|---------------|
| **Vendor Consolidation** | Reduce vendor count, aggregate volume | Fewer vendors = leverage for discounts | $9,463,275 (36.4%) |
| **Rate Negotiation** | Competitive pressure from RFP | Incumbents must match or lose | $11,044,113 (42.5%) |
| **Contract Standardization** | Standard terms, enforceable SLAs | Reduce maverick terms, ensure compliance | $5,522,057 (21.2%) |
| **TOTAL CATEGORY 1** | | | **$26,029,445** |

#### Category 2: Operational Savings ($20.0M / 15.7%)

Savings achieved through infrastructure investments (detailed in Part 5):

| Initiative | Annual Savings | Description |
|------------|----------------|-------------|
| NIM System | $12,144,000 | Nielsen Invoice Management - capture rogue spend |
| Central VMO | $6,880,000 | Vendor Management Office - operational efficiency |
| Internal Management Reduction | $1,012,500 | Fewer vendors = less management overhead |
| **TOTAL CATEGORY 2** | **$20,036,500** | |

#### Combined 2028 Run-Rate

| Category | Amount | % of $127.6M Baseline |
|----------|--------|----------------------|
| Category 1 (Direct RFP) | $26,029,445 | 20.40% |
| Category 2 (Operational) | $20,036,500 | 15.70% |
| **TOTAL SAVINGS** | **$46,065,945** | **36.10%** |

**2028 Target Spend: $81,534,197**

### 4.7 Critical Success Factors

#### For RFP Execution
- Executive sponsorship at CPO/CHRO level
- Adequate time for proper RFP process (don't rush)
- Realistic transition timelines
- Stakeholder buy-in before major vendor changes

#### For Savings Realization
- Track savings by pillar monthly
- Hold vendors to contracted rates
- Enforce consolidation (don't allow scope creep back to eliminated vendors)
- Measure compliance quarterly

#### For Risk Mitigation
- Every RFP addresses specific risks (see DUAL PURPOSE tags)
- Change management for affected employees
- Transition support for vendor switches
- Contingency plans for failed implementations
