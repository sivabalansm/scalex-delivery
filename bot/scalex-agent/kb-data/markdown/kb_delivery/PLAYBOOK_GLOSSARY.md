## A

### Account Orchestration
**Definition**: The strategic management of multiple client stakeholders (Product, Finance, Legal) to ensure the vendor is seen as a universal solution provider.

**Why It Matters for Buyers**: Use Account Orchestration to interpret vendor behavior and contract language and to decide what you should verify, require, or renegotiate.

**Why It Matters for Vendors**: Use Account Orchestration to shape selling and delivery behaviors that buyers reward: trust, clarity, and low effort.

**Related Terms**: None
**Source**: Chapter 10 - Grow a Billion Dollar Client (The Voldemort Playbook)

---

### Apex Predator Vendor
**Definition**: A provider that proactively identifies growth opportunities and embeds itself into a client’s core operations to eliminate competition.

**Why It Matters for Buyers**: Use Apex Predator Vendor to recognize when a provider is trying to become structurally embedded—capture benefits while governing lock-in risk.

**Why It Matters for Vendors**: Use Apex Predator Vendor to grow by solving messy problems, scaling what works, and adapting as the client shifts from speed to cost.

**Related Terms**: Workflow Incubation, Growth Outsourcing, Structural Indispensability
**Source**: Chapter 10 - Grow a Billion Dollar Client (The Voldemort Playbook)
